# ClanActive: The Kinetic Vault

**ClanActive** is a high-performance fitness social platform designed to turn consistency into a competitive sport. Built for athletes who thrive on accountability, the app combines atomic workout logging with real-time clan-based competition.

Developed by **Inder Baldawa** during the **Agentic Premier League** at the **Google office in Bangalore, 2026**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. The Kinetic Vault (Workout Journal)
- **Atomic Logging**: Record workouts by body part, duration, and intensity in seconds.
- **Community Feed**: A live, real-time stream of all athlete activity across the platform.
- **Private Log**: Switch to your personal journal to track your individual progress over time.

### 2. Consistency Engine
- **Streak Tracking**: Automated calculation of daily activity streaks. The system rewards "Daily Deposits" to maintain momentum.
- **Dynamic Leaderboard**: A real-time ranking system based on total volume and consistency.

### 3. Clans (Social Tribes)
- **Clan Management**: Create or join fitness "Clans" to compete together.
- **Collaborative Momentum**: High-visibility member counts and communal activity goals.

### 4. Hybrid Authentication
- **Anonymous Entry**: Join instantly with just an athlete name for a frictionless onboarding experience.
- **Google Integration**: Social login for permanent profile syncing and identity verification.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### The "Aesthetic of Performance"
The app uses a **Technical Dashboard / Data Grid** aesthetic (inspired by modern specialist tools):
- **Typography**: Inter (Sans-serif) for high legibility, paired with JetBrains Mono for technical data points like intensity and duration.
- **Layout**: A "Geometric Card" pattern using visible structures and intentional borders rather than generic shadows.
- **Visual Rhythm**: High-contrast micro-labels and uppercase tracking to reinforce a sense of precision and performance.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - **Primary**: A sharp athletic blue/navy.
  - **Background**: Soft slates and pure whites to reduce visual noise and focus on performance data.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React 18, TypeScript, Vite.
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS.
- **Animations**: Framer Motion (`motion/react`).
- **Icons**: Lucide React.
- **Backend**: Firebase 10 (Firestore, Authentication, Hosting).
- **Architecture**: Serverless event-driven sync with Firestore `onSnapshot` for real-time reactivity.
